There are many strange creatures in the world of Jujutsu Kaisen. After all, in a world where all kinds of Cursed Spirits roam the earth so freely, seeing one or two talking animals is simply part of the routine. The same can be said about Jujutsu Sorcerers, because they too come in all kinds of shapes and sizes.

The overwhelming majority of Jujutsu Sorcerers are human, but some of them certainly seem to be far removed from what you would consider normal. Mechamaru and Panda are excellent examples of this. Mechamaru is actually a Cursed Puppet controlled by Kokichi Muta from afar. But Panda is different, so who or what is he?

We are introduced to Panda when Yuji enters the Tokyo Jujutsu academy. Panda is one of Yuji's senior classmates at that time, along with Maki Zen'in, Toge Inumaki, and Yuta Okkotsu, who was absent during the first season. Needless to say, Yuji was bewildered when he first saw Panda. But since there are still many things he doesn't know about the Jujutsu world, Yuji simply takes it as one of those mysterious things he has to accept.

Panda has an incredibly optimistic and positive personality, which makes everyone who knows him feel comfortable talking and spending time with him. As a second-year student, Panda can be considered a skilled Jujutsu sorcerer. However, unlike Inumaki, who uses a specific type of cursed technique, or Maki, who fights using cursed tools, Panda is more of a fighter who uses his incredible strength and physical power in close-quarters combat, without relying on a particular cursed technique, whether innate or inherited.

In fact, Panda's main ability is more of a physical characteristic of his construction.

Both Panda and Mechamaru are considered Cursed Corpses, that is, inanimate objects that move in a particular way. However, unlike Mechamaru, who is simply a Cursed Corpse controlled by a Jujutsu Sorcerer from afar, Panda actually belongs to a category known as Abrupt Mutation Cursed Corpse.

There are no Jujutsu sorcerers controlling how he moves and what he says from afar. Panda is a completely independent and conscious Cursed Corpse. Not only that, but Panda can also generate cursed energy just like regular Jujutsu Sorcerers, which is something unheard of in the Jujutsu world.

Panda was created by the principal of the Tokyo jujutsu school, Masamichi Yaga. Yaga is the foremost expert in the Jujutsu puppetry technique, and Panda is his masterpiece. Unlike other Cursed Corpses, which usually have only one core, Panda has three different cores in his body. That is what allows him to transform into a different attack mode. Since a core is the heart of a Cursed Corpse, Panda has the ability to change the location of his cores to better protect himself.

This is why Panda has proven capable of changing his appearance on numerous occasions. His normal panda appearance is actually his default and most balanced mode. He can transform into a gorilla mode whenever he wishes, changing his appearance to resemble an enormous gorilla. Not only that, but this mode also exponentially increases his strength. In this mode, Panda can send Mechamaru flying with a single blow, and his Drum Beat is extremely powerful and can deal damage even if the opponent blocks, since the shockwaves generated by his strikes affect the internal body of whoever blocks. Panda says that the gorilla soul is the soul of his older brother, giving a certain level of personality to his technique.

On the other hand, Panda can switch to the soul of his younger sister and take on her triceratops form, in which he changes into a two-legged dinosaur form with a triceratops head. In this form Panda takes on a feminine appearance, and his personality seems to change as well, becoming more bashful and aggressive toward whoever looks at him. Unfortunately we have not seen whether this form has special techniques, but it will surely be a faster form with a powerful charge that will seek to finish off the opponent with a single blow, though it must have lower stamina and will tire quickly.

Although Panda is unusual given his level of power and his ability to generate cursed energy, there are quite a few other Cursed Corpses like him, and all of them are created by Masamichi Yaga. After creating Panda, Yaga began to perfect his technique by creating numerous conscious Cursed Corpses of all shapes and sizes, particularly in the form of dolls. And although they are not on the same level as Panda, they are also quite strong.

Some of these dolls are used as training partners for the students of the academy, while the more powerful ones are used as combat partners or even bodyguards, as during the Shibuya Incident arc. Yaga's technique has evolved to such a high level that he can not only create a conscious Cursed Corpse with its own cursed energy and personality, but he can also inject other personalities into his dolls.

One example of this is a doll named Takeru. Like Panda, it is also a conscious Cursed Corpse. However, its personality is based on a real person, specifically Kusakabe's deceased nephew. Although the doll itself does not seem to have Takeru's actual memories, it certainly has his personality. Since the Abrupt Mutation Cursed Corpse is a highly dangerous and forbidden technique, Yaga keeps most of his creations hidden. The only one that can move freely is Panda.
