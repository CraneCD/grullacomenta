The special grade in Jujutsu Kaisen is reserved for the strongest of the strong, whether sorcerers or cursed spirits, and they are extremely rare and limited, giving us a sense of just how truly special they are. Sadly, among the curses, there are few in the special grade that are truly monstrous, with most of them being basically humanoids with particular traits. However, one of my favorite cursed spirits, and one that causes terror both inside and outside the world of Jujutsu Kaisen, is Kurourushi, our topic for today.

Kurourushi is a special grade cursed spirit released by Kenjaku's Cursed Spirit Manipulation during the Culling Game arc. What makes him particular is that Kurourushi participates in the games as just another player, and in fact he has some of the most interesting fights and abilities we have seen in the arc.

Kurourushi looks like what you would expect from a cockroach cursed spirit. His face, arms, and legs have the appearance of being made from a light-colored insect exoskeleton. Kurourushi is quite tall and can produce up to four human arms. He also possesses human hands and feet, and his abdomen is completely hidden by a black cloak that extends down from his head. He generally covers his wings and extremities, but they can be seen when they move.

Kurourushi's face is grotesque and perfectly reflects the alien nature of an insect like a cockroach. With six long antennae protruding from it, Kurourushi's face has eight separate sets of eyes in different places and human-like teeth behind his multi-layered jaw.

Kurourushi does not possess a distinctive ego and is instead driven entirely by his instincts as a cursed spirit. He only wants to devour humans in an attempt to satisfy his insatiable appetite. He feels disgust toward anything that gets in the way of his feeding.

When Yuta interrupted Kurourushi's meal after he awoke in a state of hunger, the cockroach curse focused solely on devouring him instead. Kurourushi even questioned why Yuta was interfering. When Yuta responded by asking why Kurourushi kills, the cockroach curse simply stated that he loved the taste of iron.

Kurourushi is one of only sixteen registered special grade cursed spirits. Humanity shares a collective fear and disdain for cockroaches, which gave rise to Kurourushi. He possesses the unique physiology of a humanoid cockroach, as well as the ability to summon and manipulate endless cockroaches reinforced by his cursed energy. In addition, Kurourushi can use cursed techniques and wield his signature cursed tool, the Putrid Life Sword, an especially dangerous weapon. At some point in the past, Kurourushi was defeated by Suguru Geto or Kenjaku and was absorbed into Cursed Spirit Manipulation. Kenjaku released Kurourushi so that the cursed spirit could compete at an elite level in the Culling Game.

Despite generally acting on the instinct to satisfy an endless appetite, Kurourushi is a relatively intelligent creature compared to most cursed spirits. Although not to the degree of the unregistered special grades like Jogo, Mahito, Hanami, or Dagon, Kurourushi can speak and communicate clearly. He understands his own status within the Culling Game and collected fifty-two points before reaching a stalemate with the most powerful players in the Sendai colony. The cockroach curse was intelligent enough to understand that Dhruv would have been an unfavorable matchup and decided to enter a state of hibernation. Kurourushi also prepared for the worst by creating offspring that would inherit his cursed energy in case he was exorcised.

In terms of power level, Kurourushi was born from the collective hatred, fear, and resentment associated with cockroaches. These negative emotions manifested special grade levels of cursed energy. Each cockroach under Kurourushi's control is imbued with his cursed energy and possesses enough strength to devour a human being as a group. Kurourushi consistently manipulates large quantities of cockroaches while also using cursed techniques, demonstrating meticulous and innate control.

Kurourushi can produce and control countless swarms of real cockroaches reinforced by his cursed energy. These man-eating cockroaches can strip all the flesh from a group of humans in a matter of seconds. This feeds Kurourushi's endless appetite and stimulates parthenogenesis. A single cockroach poses no real threat, but a full swarm can be a serious problem even for a jujutsu sorcerer.

The flesh devoured by the cursed cockroaches stimulates Kurourushi's process of parthenogenesis, granting him the ability to produce more cockroaches. In addition, Kurourushi uses this to reproduce an identical but completely new cursed spirit offspring. To preserve Kurourushi's existence, the parent's cursed energy is passed on to the child when it dies. In a way, this makes the concept of Kurourushi, rather than the creature itself, relatively immortal, as long as he has enough time to create his offspring before being exorcised.

One particular ability he shows us is his Earth Insect Trance, a cursed technique that conjures flying insect curses carrying large sacs filled with a liquid substance that is released when they are destroyed. The substance enters the target's eyes to affect their vision, giving Kurourushi time to escape, or to attack his opponent from another angle.

Of course, Kurourushi also has his iconic cursed tool.
The Putrid Life Sword is said to be an enchanted blade that mixes life and death. It is a short, thick sword, similar to a butcher's knife, with a unique shape and a grotesque design fitting for an insect to wield. Its most prominent features are six distinct firing barrels on the front of the blade that can launch short-range projectiles at its target.

Even if the sword is blocked by the opponent's weapon, it is able to fire small eggs into the enemy's flesh to infect them. Shortly after embedding themselves in the target's skin, the eggs hatch and small insect-like curses emerge from the wound. The same effect occurs if the blade cuts the target directly as well. This is what gives Kurourushi an advantage against opponents who focus on mid-range attacks like Takako Uro, whose arm he ended up cutting off with this weapon before being exorcised himself.

Kurourushi is not really one of the cursed spirits with the greatest presence in the Culling Game arc or in the Jujutsu Kaisen series, but his abilities and especially his presence as a balance to the characters of the Sendai colony are very important to the story, and he warrants discussion, despite being simply a giant cockroach with a butcher's knife.

We cannot forget that the fact that special grade curses exist who can put up a fight not only against Uro and Ryu but against Yuta himself shows us the power level of the 16 registered special grade curses, so the existence and use of Kurourushi in the story is quite relevant, and despite not being the strongest in the world, his concept and theme are very interesting for lovers of horror and character design in Jujutsu Kaisen.

Now, on the darkest night, be careful with the corners covered in shadows, because there may be a hungry creature straight out of your nightmares hiding right there.
