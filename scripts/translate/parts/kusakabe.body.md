In Jujutsu Kaisen we always center the conversation on cursed techniques. The broken abilities that define the power of most characters, and that give versatility and creativity to Akutami's world. The exceptions to this rule are few, and most of them focus on the completely opposite side, or on heavenly restrictions that entirely remove the possibility of using techniques with cursed energy in exchange for powerful physical abilities, as with Toji and Maki. However, there is a third option that we know of in very few characters, but through which it is possible to reach a great level of power and be a fairly strong sorcerer. This is through the mastery of martial skills mixed with specialization in a type of technique that any sorcerer can learn, the Simple Domain, or more specifically the New Shadow Style mastered by Atsuya Kusakabe.

Technically, Kusakabe is a teacher at the Tokyo Jujutsu High, but it doesn't seem like his role is an important part of his character, since Kusakabe is mainly characterized by being a fearful person who is focused on his own self-preservation as his top priority. He is also willing to be deceptive to save his own skin, as was shown during the Shibuya Incident, where he repeatedly lied to Panda to keep him close for safety, while steering him away from where Gojo was sealed in order to avoid facing special grade curses. This shows that he isn't the most responsible person in the world, and he tends to worry more about himself than about his students or colleagues.

However, he also has a brave side, as was seen when he protected Miwa from Kenjaku's Uzumaki, which also shows that he is capable of setting aside his selfishness and having a heroic side as a teacher.

Kusakabe is not one of the central characters of Jujutsu Kaisen, and very little is really known about him beyond his abilities and techniques. He is the uncle of Takaba, a dead child whose personality now inhabits one of Yaga's cursed corpses, and we know that he has a younger sister whom he cares about quite a bit, but this doesn't give us much material to focus on.

Kusakabe's main point of interest is his peculiarity of being a first grade sorcerer despite not being able to use cursed techniques. This particular level of power warrants taking a closer look at him.

Despite not possessing an innate technique, Kusakabe is a respected grade 1 sorcerer and a master swordsman of the New Shadow Style. Mei Mei considered Kusakabe to be the only sorcerer of his grade who reached that rank without an innate technique. According to her, according to Kento Nanami and Satoru Gojo, Kusakabe is the strongest grade 1 sorcerer. Satoru specified that his statement might not be accurate if the sorcerers of the Three Great Families were taken into account.

Despite such praise, Kusakabe openly admits on several occasions that he has no chance of defeating a special grade curse and avoids them at all costs. However, he is completely certain that he can defeat other adversaries such as the curse users Toshihisa Negi and Manami Suda, whom he considers to be below his level.

While several jujutsu sorcerers in training were struggling to obtain Kenjaku's Prison Realm, Kusakabe stepped in just in time to save the lives of all of his allies. Even Kenjaku commented on Kusakabe's knowledge of the New Shadow Style, seemingly as a sign of respect for his skill.

Kusakabe's swordsmanship is his main weapon in battle, relying heavily on his physical prowess. In combination with the swordsmanship techniques of the New Shadow Style, he has demonstrated a high proficiency in handling his sword. Using the Battō style, Kusakabe quickly dispatched three curse user henchmen in an instant.

As a grade 1 sorcerer, Kusakabe possesses respectable levels of cursed energy. To compensate for his lack of an innate technique, Kusakabe has mastered the New Shadow Style. This requires finely controlled applications of cursed energy, such as casting a Simple Domain and coating the blade of his katana with cursed energy. Kusakabe also has a sharp sense for judging the strength of his opponent, being able to recognize someone of his level versus a special grade in an instant.

Kusakabe's Simple Domain is an application of a barrier technique to coat his body or his sword and thus increase his power, restore its form, or repel other barriers such as, for example, a domain expansion.

During the peak era of jujutsu in the Heian Era, Sadatsuna Ashiya created the "domain for the weak" with the purpose of protecting his followers from curses and curse users. Binding vows were established that prohibited teaching the Simple Domain to outsiders, but other sorcerers saw the technique in action and were able to copy it for themselves, something the vows did not account for.

Created as a counter to domain expansions, the Simple Domain neutralizes the guaranteed-hit function of a domain by affecting its barrier and not the cursed technique imbued within it. However, against a proper domain expansion, the Simple Domain can only hope to buy time for its user. Powerful domains can destroy simple domains in a matter of seconds.

The barriers of the Simple Domain can be customized with different rules and binding vows to affect those who find themselves inside it, both the caster and the target.

Kusakabe's application of the Simple Domain in his swordsmanship is similar to what we have seen with Miwa but on a much higher level; it can be said that he is the greatest expert of this application. This allowed Kusakabe to protect himself from Kenjaku's Uzumaki and from Sukuna's slashes, incredibly high feats that demonstrate the fine control he has over his barriers as well as his skill and perception.

In addition to this, Kusakabe mixes the New Shadow Style with his named sword techniques to give specific effects to his Simple Domain. These are:

Iai Battō: This technique consists of covering the blade of his katana with the barrier of the Simple Domain, increasing the speed with which the sword comes out of its sheath. This makes it the fastest technique of the New Shadow Style, and it manages to catch countless opponents off guard, as they do not expect this extreme level of speed.

Iai Night Moon Sword: This stance activates a defensive Simple Domain on Kusakabe's sword. While inside the Simple Domain, Kusakabe's cursed energy output is increased while the cursed techniques that intrude upon it are slightly weakened. By incorporating an original program into the Simple Domain, Kusakabe can automatically intercept anything that enters the Simple Domain with his katana.

Blurred Moon: With this technique Kusakabe can use the Simple Domain to reform part of his katana with cursed energy in case it is destroyed; this allows him to cover one of his greatest weaknesses, the fact that he uses a normal katana that can be destroyed. Although it is important to mention that Kusakabe's use of cursed energy raises his katana to the level of a high-level cursed tool.

As you can see, just because Kusakabe can't use an innate or inherited cursed technique, it doesn't mean he is weak; on the contrary, it is easy to underestimate him, and the speed of his techniques mixed with his mastery of the New Shadow Style are capable of taking advantage of any opening in the opponent and even finishing them off in a single blow. The mere fact that Kusakabe was able to stand against Sukuna for as long as he did makes him, as his fellow teachers said, one of the strongest first grade sorcerers to ever exist, and this with the simple mastery of a barrier technique and his skills with the sword
