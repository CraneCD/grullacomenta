One of the most interesting aspects of Jujutsu Kaisen is its Heavenly Restrictions. These particular traits of certain characters are so striking that they manifest in every aspect of their techniques and even in their personalities themselves. We have seen several examples where such restrictions partially or absolutely strip characters of their cursed energy and replace it with physical abilities and superhuman perception; however, there is only one example of the opposite, a Heavenly Restriction that leaves the person completely physically invalid and, as a consequence, grants them an absurd reserve of cursed energy and the ability to store it indefinitely. Of course, the character with this terrible restriction is Mechamaru.

Kokichi Muta, also known as Mechamaru, is a second-year student at the Kyoto Jujutsu School and one of the key characters in the development of Jujutsu Kaisen's plot. As we mentioned before, he was born with a fragile and weak body due to his Heavenly Restriction, and in exchange his reserve of cursed energy is absurdly large.

Sadly, as a result, Kokichi was unable to attend the Jujutsu School in his human form, but instead used a substitute known as Ultimate Mechamaru, a cursed corpse-like robot controlled by his Puppet Manipulation technique, which he can command at will.

Kokichi is initially shown with wrinkled skin and covered in bandages, lying in a bathtub with tubes around him keeping him alive. He is missing his right arm and has no sensation below the waist. In addition, he has extremely sensitive skin that cannot be exposed to outside air. In exchange for this, Kokichi has a vast amount of stored cursed energy and primarily uses his Puppet Manipulation technique to control Mechamaru and his other puppets. Kokichi is for the most part a calm character who hates having been born and would do anything to lead a normal human life. He compares himself to Panda, who is also a cursed corpse like Mechamaru, which enrages him, as he is jealous that Panda can explore the world while he is trapped inside Mechamaru's consciousness.

Kokichi simply wanted to live a normal life with his classmates as an ordinary human jujutsu sorcerer. Later, it becomes clear that his desperation knew no bounds, as he ends up betraying his friends by making a binding vow with Mahito and Kenjaku to provide vital information in exchange for a healthy body. However, he asks them not to harm his friends, but as soon as the binding vow was completed, a battle with Mahito breaks out. Kokichi, realizing that he needs to protect his classmates, plunges into an all-out battle with Mahito, during which he excellently showed us the levels he was able to reach with his cursed technique, tactical intelligence, mechatronic skill, and reserve of cursed energy.

Innate Cursed Technique: Puppet Manipulation

Kokichi's technique allowed him to remotely control puppets or cursed corpses across a wide range. Puppets such as Mechamaru are capable of using cursed energy discharge as heat rays, among a variety of other abilities.

Having been paralyzed for most of his life, Kokichi transferred his consciousness into Mechamaru's mechanical body, which gave him the ability to move, interact with his colleagues, and also granted him combat abilities. Despite his physical limitations, Kokichi was able to fight effectively against curses and curse users by using Mechamaru to his advantage. Equipped with devices and weapons that enhance Kokichi's jujutsu abilities, Mechamaru was capable of firing cursed energy in the form of high-intensity beams from his palms, transforming his arms into deadly weapons and powerful shields.

Thanks to Kokichi's Puppet Manipulation technique, he was able to control multiple Mechamarus wherever they were. Mechamaru also has an even more powerful move known as Mode: Albatross, which allows him to reveal a cannon inside his robotic mouth capable of firing an immense beam of cursed energy from the barrel, destroying everything in its path.

During Kokichi's final battle, he used Ultimate Mechamaru-Mode: Absolute, a gigantic mechanical puppet that Kokichi can control from the cockpit located in its head. Of course, we fans know that the abilities and combat style of this version of Mechamaru are a subtle nod by Gege to the legendary Evangelion.

The gigantic mechanical puppet also had its own set of powerful attacks, such as the Miracle Cannon, a powerful burst of energy from both hands, and the Dove Viola, the cursed energy version of guided missiles, which follow targets until impact. Kokichi's Heavenly Restriction also plays a key role here, as he was able to gather a vast amount of cursed energy consisting of seventeen years, five months, and six days of cursed energy accumulated throughout his life, allowing him to unleash powerful attacks all at once.

As for barrier techniques, Kokichi was able to develop a technique known as the New Shadow Style: Simple Domain. He executes this by firing a sharp bullet from the giant puppet's finger; once the bullet enters the opponent's body, Kokichi can activate his Simple Domain cursed technique.

Kokichi is also known for being a great strategist who can assess his opponent and take advantage of his surroundings; even Kenjaku praised him for his tactical intellect during his fight with Mahito. Kokichi continually came close to defeating Mahito and damaging his body.

During the Shibuya Incident, although dead, Kokichi appears in the form of a small circular mechanical device, consisting of the final remnants of his cursed energy. He used his remaining cursed energy to help his classmates navigate the surrounding dangers and also supported them during their battles against the enemies.

We have seen few intellects like Kokichi's, and even though he is no longer with us, we can praise his character as one of the most important supporting characters in all of Jujutsu Kaisen, giving him the spotlight during much of the Shibuya Incident arc, vindicating him in the minds of many and raising him up the ranks of the fans' favorite characters.
